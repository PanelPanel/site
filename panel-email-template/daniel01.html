ALEXANDRA EASTBURN, CHIMERA SEDUCTION, 2017

Daniel Long and Alexandra Eastburn
Pamper Rooms
April 14 - May 21

Opening Thursday
April 13, 7PM


PANEL L.A. is proud to present Pamper Rooms, a joint exhibition of new works from Daniel Long and Alexandra Eastburn. This marks the first collaboration between the artists and is the culmination of a six week residency.

Long and Eastburn have created a series of environments within the gallery. Drawing inspiration from various architectures including spas and sex dungeons, Pamper Rooms portrays ideas of cruising, masks, and the duplicitous nature of self (care). Looking into the bathroom mirror is not unlike a dungeon, both a private and exposed experience, one is inclined to indulge in the intimate while ultimately tending to public appearance.

Curated by Chiara Giovando, exhibition design developed with Armbay Zakaria and Neil Doshi

Save the date for a special screening of Sam Hamilton’s Apple Pie
April 27, 7PM, as part of Pamper Rooms.

PANEL L.A. is a space for contemporary art in Los Angeles California, engaged in an experiment that hopes to support artists' practices holistically, and to bring process and collective interests more directly into the event of the exhibition.
